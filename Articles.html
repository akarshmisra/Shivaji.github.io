Shivaji: Additional Information

JADUNATH SARKAR, Shivaji and His Times, 6th ed., rev. and enlarged (1961), is a study based chiefly on Persian sources. Other biographies of Shivaji include H.G. RAWLINSON, Shivájí the Maráthá: His Life and Times (1915, reprinted 1984); N.S. TAKAKHAV, The Life of Shivaji Mahari, Founder of the Maratha Empire (1921, reissued as Life of Shivaji, 2 vol. (1985), based mainly on Marathi sources; and V.B. KULKARNI, Shivaji: The Portrait of a Patriot (1963).
